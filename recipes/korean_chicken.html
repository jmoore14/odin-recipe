Korean Chicken Recipe

Description

This healthy korean chicken dish will surely be a hit with everyone. Its a healthy alternative to the usually deep fried version but just as spicy and succulent thanks to the marinade.

Make sure you let the chicken marinade for 4 - 24 hours in advance!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs

Marinade

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1.5 tbsp Gochujang
  • 1 tsp Gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 knob of minced ginger
  • 3 close garlic, minced
Steps
  1. Slice the chicken thighs horizontally to make thinner pieces of meat. Slice each piece of chicken thigh (depends on its thickness) into 2 or 3 horizontal pieces. Set aside.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in the Marinade in a bowl, whisk it to make sure the Korean chili paste (gochujang) is completely dissolved. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours.
  3. Grill the chicken until slightly charred and completely cooked through. You may also pan-fried the chicken on a skillet or pan, or bake in the oven at 400°F (207°C) for about 20 minutes. Serve immediately with steamed rice.
