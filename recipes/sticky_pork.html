Sticky Pork

Description

These sticky pork belly bites are to die for and please listen to my entire life story

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g Pork Belly
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp finely grated ginger
  • 1 spring onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Cook pork for 5 minutes or until golden brown on each side
  2. Add the soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce, honey, garlic and ginger to the pan. Cook, tossing, for 3-5 mins or until the pork is coated in sauce and the sauce is sticky.
  3. Arrange the pork on a serving plate. Drizzle with a little sauce and sprinkle with spring onion and chilli, if using.
